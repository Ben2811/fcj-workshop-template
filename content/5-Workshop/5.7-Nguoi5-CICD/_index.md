In the PeriodIQ project, Pham Van Sy is responsible for the CI/CD pipeline and monitoring infrastructure. In production, these services are wired together by AWS SAM/CloudFormation and a CodePipeline pipeline. For this workshop, each service is rebuilt manually, step by step, with the AWS CLI only, to show exactly what SAM/CodePipeline actually provision under the hood.
