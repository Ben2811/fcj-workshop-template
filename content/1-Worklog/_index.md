I completed the **Workforce Bootcamp – First Cloud AI Journey (FCJ)** internship program at **Amazon Web Services Vietnam Co., Ltd.**, over **12 weeks** from 17/04/2026 to 10/07/2026. Most of the 12 weeks followed the AWS Study Group curriculum to build up core AWS skills. In parallel, my team kicked off our real project, **PeriodIQ**, in Week 6 and split into 5 role groups; I picked up light project work alongside the curriculum in Week 7, then spent Weeks 10–12 building out my **Admin Panel & Data** role end-to-end and writing up the resulting AWS Workshop chapter of this report.

Below is a week-by-week summary of what I did:

**Week 1:** [AWS account setup, IAM, and VPC fundamentals](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [EC2 deep dive, Cloud9, and RDS](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [Hybrid DNS, migration, and backup strategies](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [Advanced networking and CloudWatch monitoring](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [Infrastructure as Code and identity governance](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [Cloud security, KMS, and containers — plus the PeriodIQ team kickoff](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Hybrid storage, Data Lake, and DynamoDB modeling — plus an early PeriodIQ data model sketch](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [Cost optimization and microservices migration](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [Messaging, SPA auth, and Step Functions](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [EKS, CI/CD, and SAM — plus expanding the PeriodIQ DynamoDB design](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [Building the PeriodIQ Admin Panel & Data role end-to-end](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [Checking the frontend and writing the AWS Workshop chapter](1.12-week12/)
